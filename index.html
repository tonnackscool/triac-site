03 — Trust & security

Designed to remove itself from the equation

The most common risks in new token launches are all structurally addressed here.

✓

Supply can never inflate

Mint authority was disabled on-chain after the initial mint. This is irreversible — verifiable directly on Solscan.

✓

No wallet can be frozen

Freeze authority was never enabled on this token — holder funds cannot be locked by anyone.

✓

Liquidity will be locked or burned

When the pool is live, LP tokens will be burned or time-locked so liquidity can't be silently withdrawn.

✓

60% of treasury is time-locked

12,570,000 TRI is locked via Streamflow until October 2038, verifiable on-chain — this portion cannot be moved or sold before then, by anyone.

✓

Staking is live

Holders can stake TRI for ~5% APY at stake.smithii.io/triac, funded from a dedicated treasury allocation.

✓

Allocation is disclosed, not hidden

Treasury and team holdings will be published with wallet addresses before trading opens.
